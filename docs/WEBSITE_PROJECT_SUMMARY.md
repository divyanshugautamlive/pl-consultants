## 🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM AT A GLANCE

### Color Palette (Carefully Chosen for Manufacturing Consulting)
```
🔵 Midnight Navy (#0E2A47)      → Trust, stability, professionalism
💛 Gold Accent (#E2A23B)         → ROI, value, high-impact results
🧿 Sage Teal (#028090)           → Operations, process, improvement
⚙️ Steel Gray (#36454F)          → Professional, serious, data-driven
```

**Why These Colors?**
- Navy dominates: Trust-building for manufacturing sector
- Gold accents: Emphasizes profit recovery (rupee values, ROI)
- Teal: Process improvement & operations focus
- Gray: Professional hierarchy, readability

### Typography System
- **Headings:** Cambria/Georgia serif (professional, manufacturing trust)
- **Body:** Calibri/Arial sans-serif (clean, readable, web-standard)
- **Stats:** Large bold gold numbers (₹ values, impact metrics emphasized)
- **Line Heights:** Generous (1.6 body, 1.2 headings) for readability

### Responsive Design
- Mobile-first approach (design for mobile, enhance for desktop)
- Breakpoints: sm (640px) | md (768px) | lg (1024px) | xl (1280px)
- Touch targets: 44×44px minimum

## 🎯 CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

### 1. **Color Consistency**
Use the exact hex codes provided. Navy (#0E2A47) should be everywhere—headers, CTAs, backgrounds. Gold (#E2A23B) should highlight ROI metrics only (not overused).

### 2. **Messaging Discipline**
- Always use rupees, not percentages
- Lead with business impact, not process
- Emphasize hands-on execution (Tarun-led)
- Quantify everything

### 3. **Case Study Leverage**
Every case study is a conversion asset. Include:
- Specific challenge
- Methodology steps taken
- Before/after metrics
- ₹ impact (prominently displayed in gold box)
- Timeline
- Client quote (if approved)

### 4. **Form Optimization**
- Simple form (5-6 fields max)
- Clear CTA text ("Start Assessment")
- WhatsApp link always available
- Lead notifications to Tarun within 1 hour

### 5. **SEO Foundation**
- Keyword focus: "manufacturing consulting," "operational excellence," "OEE," "Lean Six Sigma"
- Blog posts targeting long-tail keywords
- Internal linking strategy (services → blog)
- Regular content updates (2-3 posts/month)

### 6. **Responsive Mobile**
- Design for mobile-first
- Test on real devices (not just browser DevTools)
- Touch targets 44×44px minimum
- Fast mobile load time (<3s)
