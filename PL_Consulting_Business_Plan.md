## SECTION 2: SERVICE OFFERINGS & VALUE PROPOSITION

### 2.1 Core Services

**Service Model: 5-Phase Engagement ("DADES")**

**Phase 1: DIAGNOSE (Weeks 1–2)**
- Shopfloor study and data capture
- Day-In-the-Life-Of (DILO) analysis
- OEE assessment (Overall Equipment Effectiveness)
- Loss mapping and waste identification
- Deliverable: Diagnostic report identifying losses

**Phase 2: ANALYZE (Weeks 2–4)**
- Root-cause analysis of bottlenecks
- Capacity and throughput studies
- Workforce balancing assessment
- Lead-time value stream analysis
- Financial quantification of each loss
- Deliverable: Detailed analysis report with rupee-denominated losses

**Phase 3: DESIGN (Weeks 3–5, parallel to Analyze)**
- Solution roadmap development
- SMED (Single Minute Exchange of Dies) design
- Line balancing & layout redesign
- Workforce optimization plan
- Working capital reduction strategy
- Business case and ROI model
- Deliverable: Implementation roadmap + business case

**Phase 4: IMPLEMENT (Weeks 6–12, typically 8–10 weeks)**
- Pilot line execution with client team
- SMED trials and standard work
- Line balancing & layout changes (if needed)
- Workforce redeployment
- Quick wins delivery
- KPI dashboard setup
- Deliverable: Implemented improvements, visible floor changes

**Phase 5: SUSTAIN (Weeks 10–16, ongoing basis)**
- Standard work documentation
- Visual management systems
- KPI monitoring and variance analysis
- Capability building of client teams
- Quarterly reviews and continuous improvement cycles
- Deliverable: Sustained gains, self-sufficient client operations team
